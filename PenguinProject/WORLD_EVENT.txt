1. To trigger the event, you only need to click on any unoccupied space.
2. When triggered, the pressed square and cardinal neighbors background should change to seaweed,
    a shark will appear, and the nearest fish will turn into a bird.
3. The nearest fish turns into, and acts, like the bird entity.
4. The new entity is a shark, which chases the player (penguin). If close, the shark attacks the
    player's health until it is zero, and then prints the message "game over".
